## 🎮 How to Play

### Desktop
- **Keys:** `1-6` or `Q-I`
- **Pause:** `Space`
- **Goal:** Hit notes when they reach the white line

### Mobile
- **Touch:** Tap the colored piano keys
- **Goal:** Timing is everything!

### Scoring
| Hit | Points | Window |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Perfect 💎 | 100 | ±30ms |
| Good ✅ | 50 | ±80ms |
| Bad ⚠️ | 10 | ±150ms |

**Combo Multiplier:** 5x = 1.5x, 10x+ = 2x
